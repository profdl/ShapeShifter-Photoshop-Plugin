INSTRUCTIONS:
To create a generative animation:
  • 1. Choose a preset.
  • 2. Repeatedly click the "Create Animation Frame" button to generate images on separate layers.
  • 3. As you animate, you can choose generators to add, and you can adjust the settings for each. This will vary the animation effects. Of course, you can also modify frames using photoshop tools such as the paintbrush and image adjustments.
  • 4. Once you are finished creating images for your animation, click "Create Animation Timeline". In the top menu select Window > Timeline. Save your animation video file.

    • Tips:
    • I typically start with a 1024x1024px canvas with black background.
    • I then add images on top of this before I start animating, but you can also just start with a blank canvas.
    • I export my animation to video every 300 layers or so. Then create a new document from the last frame / top layer.
    • Also, don't forget that you can modify your frames with tools such as paintbrush (layer-adjustments, copying/pasting, etc.) as you go.
